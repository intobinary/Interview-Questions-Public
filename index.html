About

"Practice Interview Questions" is a simple tool that can help you improve your interviewing skills.

If you're looking for a job and want to present fluently and confidently, you can train by speaking on a variety of different topics.

It just gives you an IT topic. All you need to do is to tell something about the given topic. You can practice with youself or with a partner.

If you don't like the topic just skip it and try another one. Also you can use a timer to change topics automatically.

If you do this exercise on a regular basis, it will expand your interviewing skills.

You can use it when you are jogging or driving a car or washing dishes or whatever.
